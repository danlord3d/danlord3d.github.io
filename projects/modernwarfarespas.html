This project was developed over the course of 6 weeks as part of a University assignment for the module "Hard Surface Modelling".

Throughout the course of this module I learnt about:

  • The Allegorithmic Substance Painter Toolset.
  • Using industry references to create an industry standard weapo model.
  • Learnt how to use the Quixel suite, specifically the NDO Painter.
