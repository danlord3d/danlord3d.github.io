MEDIEVAL KITCHEN

This project was developed over the course of 12 weeks as part of a University assignment for the module "Mastering the Artistry of Games Environments". Throughout the course of this module I learnt about:

  • The use of decals to produce good edgewear on the walls.
  • How to setup professional grade lighting using the Unreal 4 Engine.
  • How to lightmap a scene correctly using 3DS Max
  • Further progressed my technical ability using 3DS Max, the Substance Suite, Adobe Photoshop and Unreal Engine 4.
