Sci-Fi Corridor

This project was developed over the course of 12 weeks as part of a University assignment for the module "Environment Modelling".

Throughout the course of this module I learnt about:

  • How to effectively use trim sheets to create a modular environment.
  • Got introduced to lightmapping to produce higher quality shading in my environment.
  • Learnt how to utilize vertex painting to create edgeware and grunge on the environment models.
